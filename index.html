Alexander Deeley

From molding my first clay creations at the age of 4 with my grandmother, Florence Deeley, to taking up drumming as an 8 year old and photography at 10 - art and design have always been an integral part of who I am.

My first design internship started in 2001 at Loaded Media, a Philadelphia based digital marketing agency. I was introduced to Adobe + Macromedia products and object-oriented programming . I rose to be lead developer at Benzing Technologies and also at Claris Legal Marketing, both located in sunny Florida.

These days my main focus is building robust & effective web experiences over at Brainstorm + developing the server-infrastructure at PRI5M.
